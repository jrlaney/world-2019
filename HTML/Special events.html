1:1 MicroStrategy 2019 Consultation

Excited about MicroStrategy 2019, but weary of the process to upgrade? Meet our upgrade experts to maximize your MicroStrategy 2019 experience. Learn more about how your organization can take advantage of the powerful new functionalities available since the last platform release, including HyperIntelligence, the enterprise semantic graph, dossiers, library and much more.

In addition to getting tips on how to prepare for a seamless upgrade process, the MicroStrategy expert will also talk about new features like testing automation in the platform that help you upgrade. We’ll also discuss how you can schedule a complimentary one-week Upgrade Advisory--where we send an expert to your location to help conduct sandbox upgrading testing, build and deploy a HyperIntelligence pilot, and provide a detailed feature value map and clear recommendations for a smooth upgrade.
