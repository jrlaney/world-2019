What’s New in 2019

All user roles can take this class to use the new MicroStrategy 2019 features to explore the connection between an agile environment and the departmental/enterprise environment, within the context of data governance. Use MicroStrategy Workstation to understand the effects of administrative tasks that maximize the value of your MicroStrategy environments and applications. Ensure data integrity and security while you monitor and analyze your environments, users, and data with Platform Analytics. Empower your sales people and executives by connecting to your data with HyperIntelligence, which uses augmented analytics to display information cards on web pages. Write back to your data with actionable analytics, so that decision makers have the information that they need to make the right decision.

Education event

Transform Your Data-Driven Organization: The Intelligent Enterprise

To navigate and thrive in an increasingly complex BI datascape, forward-leaning companies are racing to transform into an Intelligent Enterprise – capable of using data, people, and enterprise assets in aggregated and unique ways. In this class, evaluate where your organization stands today, understand how to establish a foundation for growth, and drive Business Intelligence adoption within your enterprise. Topics covered include data governance, process and change management, enterprise mobility, building your team, and empowering users. At the end of this course, you will be armed with the tools to start transforming your organization into an Intelligent Enterprise as you advance on your personal and enterprise BI journey.
