Building a More Intelligent Enterprise: Best Practices for the Intelligence Director

Education event

To navigate and thrive in an increasingly complex BI datascape, forward-leaning companies are racing to transform into Intelligent Enterprises—capable of using data, people, and assets in aggregated and unique ways. In this class, you’ll evaluate where your organization stands today, understand how to establish a foundation for growth, and learn how to drive business intelligence adoption within your enterprise. This class will cover topics like data governance, process and change management, enterprise mobility, how to build your team, and how to empower users.

This course is ideal for those running a BI group—directors, VPs, and executives looking to establish a foundation for growing a successful enterprise with the MicroStrategy platform. Learn what a fully scalable Intelligent Enterprise can do when it’s aligned with corporate standards and staffed with experts using best practices in their daily work. Transform your BI and become a change advocate for intelligent, competitive analytics in your organization.

Comprehensive Overview and Hands-On Experience With MicroStrategy 2019

Education event

MicroStrategy 2019 is the world’s first and only platform for federated analytics, transformation mobility, and HyperIntelligence. It is the most open and complete offering on the market—delivering modern analytics on an enterprise platform that can be deployed on multiple private or public cloud platforms —designed to help make every enterprise a more Intelligent Enterprise. This course is ideal for analysts, business users, developers, data scientists, architects, or administrators seeking a comprehensive overview of the amazing new capabilities in MicroStrategy 2019. Topics include:

  • Modern Analytics: Highlights include the modern MicroStrategy Dossier and Library experience, natural language capabilities, geospatial analytics and interactive collaboration options.
  • Open Architecture: Highlights include MicroStrategy on AWS, MicroStrategy on Azure, and deep dive into REST APIs to build and deploy custom applications.
  • Enterprise Platform: Highlights include MicroStrategy Workstation and Platform Analytics to monitor application usage in real-time, manager user security, build and tune applications for high performance and scale.
  • Federated Analytics: Highlights include an overview of the Enterprise Semantic Graph, a common business glossary and metadata index for enterprise assets and how to leverage MicroStrategy for trusted analytics with Tableau, Qlik, PowerBI, R Studio and Jupyter Notebook.
  • Transformational Mobility: Highlights include mobile dossiers, no-code drag-and-drop and customized development with SDKs.
  • HyperIntelligence: Highlights include HyperCards, integration with Alexa, Chatbots and other augmented reality apps that enable HyperIntelligence, a new category of analytics that imbues every word, object, place, room, and wall with zero-click personalized Intelligence.
