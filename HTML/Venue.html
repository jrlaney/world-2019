Venue & Accommodations

This year MicroStrategy World will be hosted at The Phoenix Convention Center.

 
 
 
 
 

Venue

Phoenix Convention Center

The Phoenix Convention Center is located in the heart of downtown Phoenix and just 4 miles from Sky Harbor International Airport. The center is within walking distance of major convention hotels, shopping, entertainment, sports, theater venues and has its own dedicated stop on the Metro Light Rail line.

Accommodations

Hyatt Regency Phoenix

Located across the street from the Phoenix Convention Center, Hyatt Regency Phoenix places downtown Phoenix at your feet.

Discounted room blocks for World 2019 attendees are available. To reserve a room, book online or call the Hyatt Regency Phoenix hotel at 602.252.1234 and let them know you are a MicroStrategy World attendee. Rooms at the preferred rate are available and are based upon availability.
