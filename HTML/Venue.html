Phoenix Convention Center

The Phoenix Convention Center is located in the heart of downtown Phoenix and just 4 miles from Sky Harbor International Airport. The center is within walking distance of major convention hotels, shopping, entertainment, sports, theater venues and has its own dedicated stop on the Metro Light Rail line.

Air Travel

The Phoenix Convention Center is four miles from Sky Harbor International Airport. Guests flying into Phoenix have multiple transportation options, including the Metro Light Rail, which has dedicated stops at the Convention Center.

Places to check out while at World 2019:

 
 
 

Grand Canyon

Take in unforgettable views of classic southwestern landscapes. Explore the Grand Canyon South Rim in this incredible tour from Phoenix that covers the deepest and widest parts of the Grand Canyon.

 
 
 
 

Sedona

Tour the magical red rocks of Sedona and visit Native American ruins during this Sedona Red Rocks Tour from Phoenix.

 
 
 

Antelope Canyon

Tour Antelope Canyon in the only day-tour available from the Phoenix area! Experience one of the most picturesque slot canyons before taking in the breathtaking views of Horseshoe Bend.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Tombstone

On this guided day tour of Tombstone, Arizona, explore the “Town Too Tough To Die” and its classic western history like the OK Corral.

 
 
 
 

The City

Explore one of the most diverse cities in the nation. See downtown Phoenix, the time-honored Arizona capitol building, Old Town Scottsdale and the historic Biltmore architecture as we tour the Valley of the Sun.
